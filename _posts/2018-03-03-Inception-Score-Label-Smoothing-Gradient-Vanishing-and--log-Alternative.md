##### Abstract
In this article, we mathematically study several GAN related topics, including Inception score, label smoothing, gradient vanishing and the -log(D(x)) alternative. 
 --- An advanced version is included in <a href="http://export.arxiv.org/abs/1703.02000">arXiv:1703.02000</a> "Activation Maximization Generative Adversarial Nets". Please refer Section 6 in <a href="http://export.arxiv.org/abs/1703.02000">1703.02000</a> for detailed analysis on Inception Score, and refer its appendix for the discussions on Label Smoothing, Gradient Vanishing and -log(D(x)) Alternative. ---
