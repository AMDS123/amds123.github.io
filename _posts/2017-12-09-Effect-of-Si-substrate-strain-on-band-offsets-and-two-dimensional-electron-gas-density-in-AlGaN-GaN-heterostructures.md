##### Abstract
The effects of strain in the GaN layer on band gaps, band offsets and 2DEG density in pseudomorphic AlGaN-on-GaN heterostructures have been computed using a combination of empirical tight-binding bandstructure calculation and self-consistent Schroedinger-Poisson. It is found that while the strain induces insignificant change in band offsets, it can enhance the 2DEG density substantially for low Al mole fraction - by as much as 25%.
