##### Abstract
Imagine taking a selfie video with your mobile phone and getting as output a 3D model of your head (face and 3D hair strands) that can be later used in VR, AR, and any other domain. State of the art hair reconstruction methods allow either a single photo (thus compromising 3D quality) or multiple views, but they require manual user interaction (manual hair segmentation and capture of fixed camera views that span full 360 degree). In this paper, we describe a system that can completely automatically create a reconstruction from any video (even a selfie video), and we don't require specific views, since taking your -90 degree, 90 degree, and full back views is not feasible in a selfie capture. 
 In the core of our system, in addition to the automatization components, hair strands are estimated and deformed in 3D (rather than 2D as in state of the art) thus enabling superior results. We provide qualitative, quantitative, and Mechanical Turk human studies that support the proposed system, and show results on a diverse variety of videos (8 different celebrity videos, 9 selfie mobile videos, spanning age, gender, hair length, type, and styling).

想象一下，用手机拍摄自拍视频，并将头部（脸部和3D发束）的3D模型作为输出，以后可用于VR，AR和任何其他领域。现有技术的头发重建方法允许单个照片（因此损害3D质量）或多个视图，但是它们需要手动用户交互（手动头发分割和捕获跨越360度的固定相机视图）。在本文中，我们描述了一个系统，它可以完全自动地从任何视频（甚至自拍视频）创建重建，我们不需要特定的视图，因为你的-90度，90度和全后视图不是在拍摄自拍时可行。
 在我们系统的核心中，除了自动化组件之外，头发线被估计并且在3D中变形（而不是像现有技术那样的2D），从而实现优异的结果。我们提供定性，定量和机械土耳其人类研究，支持所提议的系统，并显示各种视频的结果（8个不同的名人视频，9个自拍移动视频，跨越年龄，性别，头发长度，类型和样式） 。
