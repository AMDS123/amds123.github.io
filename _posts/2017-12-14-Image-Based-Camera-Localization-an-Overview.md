##### Abstract
Recently, virtual reality, augmented reality, robotics, self-driving cars et al attract much attention of industrial community, in which image based camera localization is a key task. It is urgent to give an overview of image based camera localization. In this paper, an overview of image based camera localization is presented. It will be useful to not only researchers but also engineers and other people interested.
