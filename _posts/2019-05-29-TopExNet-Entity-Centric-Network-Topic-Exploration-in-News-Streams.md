##### Abstract
The recent introduction of entity-centric implicit network represen-tations of unstructured text offers novel ways for exploring entityrelations in document collections and streams efficiently and inter-actively. Here, we present TopExNet as a tool for exploring entity-centric network topics in streams of news articles. The application isavailable as a web service at https://topexnet.ifi.uni-heidelberg.de/ .
