##### Abstract
Finding Tiny Faces by Hu and Ramanan - and released at CVPR 2017 - proposes a novel approach to find small objects in an image. Our contribution consists in deeply understanding the choices of the paper together with applying and extending a similar method to a real world subject which is the counting of people in a public demonstration.
