##### Abstract
A minimalist constructed language (conlang) is useful for experiments and comfortable for making tools. The Toki Pona (TP) conlang is minimalist both in the vocabulary (with only 14 letters and 124 words) and in the (about) ten syntax rules. The language is useful for being a used and somewhat established minimalist conlang with at least hundreds of fluent speakers. In this article, we describe current concepts and resources for TP, and make available Python scripted routines for the analysis of the language, the synthesis of texts, the specification of syntax highlighting schemes, and the achievement of a preliminary TP Wordnet. We focus on the analysis of the basic vocabulary, as corpus analyses were found. The synthesis is based on sentence templates, relates to context by keeping track of used words, and renders larger texts by using a fixed number of phonemes (e.g. for poems) and number of sentences, words and letters (e.g. for paragraphs). Syntax highlighting reflects morphosyntactic classes given in the official dictionary and different solutions are described and implemented in the well-established Vim text editor. The tentative TP Wordnet is made available in three forms that reflect the choices of the synsets related to each word. In summary, this text holds potentially novel conceptualizations about, and tools and results in analyzing, synthesizing and syntax highlighting the TP language.

极简主义构造语言（conlang）对于实验很有用，并且适合制作工具。 Toki Pona（TP）conlang在词汇表（只有14个字母和124个单词）和（大约）十个语法规则中都是极简主义的。这种语言对于成为一个使用过的，有点建立的极简主义的conlang很有用，至少有数百个流利的说话者。在本文中，我们描述了TP的当前概念和资源，并为语言分析，文本合成，语法突出显示方案的规范以及初步TP Wordnet的实现提供了可用的Python脚本例程。我们专注于基本词汇的分析，因为发现了语料库分析。合成基于句子模板，通过跟踪使用过的单词与上下文相关，并通过使用固定数量的音素（例如诗歌）和句子，单词和字母（例如段落）的数量来呈现更大的文本。语法高亮反映了官方词典中给出的morphosyntactic类，并且在完善的Vim文本编辑器中描述和实现了不同的解决方案。暂定的TP Wordnet以三种形式提供，反映了与每个单词相关的同义词集的选择。总之，本文包含有关TP语言分析，综合和语法突出的潜在新概念，以及工具和结果。
