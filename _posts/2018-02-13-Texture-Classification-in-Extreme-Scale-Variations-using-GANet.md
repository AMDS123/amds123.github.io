##### Abstract
Research in texture recognition often concentrates on recognizing textures with intraclass variations such as illumination, rotation, viewpoint and small scale changes. In contrast, in real-world applications a change in scale can have a dramatic impact on texture appearance, to the point of changing completely from one texture category to another. As a result, texture variations due to changes in scale are amongst the hardest to handle. In this work we conduct the first study of classifying textures with extreme variations in scale. To address this issue, we first propose and then reduce scale proposals on the basis of dominant texture patterns. Motivated by the challenges posed by this problem, we propose a new GANet network where we use a Genetic Algorithm to change the units in the hidden layers during network training, in order to promote the learning of more informative semantic texture patterns. Finally, we adopt a FVCNN (Fisher Vector pooling of a Convolutional Neural Network filter bank) feature encoder for global texture representation. 
 Because extreme scale variations are not necessarily present in most standard texture databases, to support the proposed extreme-scale aspects of texture understanding we are developing a new dataset, the Extreme Scale Variation Textures (ESVaT), to test the performance of our framework. It is demonstrated that the proposed framework significantly outperforms gold-standard texture features by more than 10% on ESVaT. We also test the performance of our proposed approach on the KTHTIPS2b and OS datasets and a further dataset synthetically derived from Forrest, showing superior performance compared to the state of the art.

纹理识别研究通常集中于识别具有类内变化的纹理，例如照明，旋转，视点和小尺度变化。相比之下，在现实世界的应用中，尺寸的变化会对纹理外观产生巨大的影响，从完全从一个纹理类别变化到另一个纹理类别。因此，由于规模变化造成的纹理变化是最难处理的。在这项工作中，我们进行了第一次对纹理进行分类的研究，这些研究的规模极其不同。为了解决这个问题，我们首先提出并根据主导纹理模式减少比例建议。受到这个问题带来的挑战的启发，我们提出了一种新的GANet网络，我们使用遗传算法在网络训练期间改变隐藏层中的单元，以促进更多信息语义纹理模式的学习。最后，我们采用用于全局纹理表示的FVCNN（卷积神经网络滤波器组的Fisher导航池）特征编码器。
 由于在大多数标准纹理数据库中不一定存在极端的尺度变化，为了支持所提出的纹理理解的极限尺度方面，我们正在开发一个新的数据集Extreme Scale Variation Textures（ESVaT）来测试我们的框架的性能。结果表明，所提出的框架在ESVaT上显着优于金标准纹理特征超过10％。我们还测试了我们建议的方法在KTHTIPS2b和OS数据集上的性能以及从Forrest合成得到的另一个数据集，与现有技术相比显示出优越的性能。
